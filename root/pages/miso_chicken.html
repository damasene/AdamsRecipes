Miso-glaze roast chicken

If you eat meat, it's hard not to love roast chicken — but this miso, butter, and honey glaze takes it to the next level. This is adapted from lots of recipes available online.

Serves 4

Ingredients

  • Medium-sized chicken (3-4 lbs)
  • 1.5 tbsp red or white miso
  • 1.5 tbsp softened butter
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1-2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • Squeeze of lemon juice (1-2 teaspoons)
  • 2 garlic cloves

Preheat the oven to 375f.

Crush the garlic or chop it finely if you don't have a garlic crusher. Put all the ingredients except the chicken into a bowl and blend into a loose paste. A fork is usually the best tool for this.

If the chicken's legs are tied together, untie them. With a butter knife or silicone spatula, spread the miso-butter all over the chicken, inside and out. Really get in there.

Roast at 375f for about 35 minutes, basting twice. Turn the oven up to 420f for the last 10-15 minutes. But your oven might be hotter or cooler than mine so check often and adjust accordingly.
